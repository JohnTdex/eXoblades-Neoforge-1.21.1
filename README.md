eXoBlades
=====================================

Ever wanted a futuristic weaponry that is beyond Netherite and diamond?
Well, this mod is just that!

eXoBlades is introducing new variety of Tools and Armor each with unique effects, aesthetic and progression. Becoming overwhelmingly powerful
against mobs be it modded or vanilla that are terrifyingly strong. Go on, embark a journey to gather new ores to craft these powerful
weapons to defend your friends, pet, villagers and yourself!
